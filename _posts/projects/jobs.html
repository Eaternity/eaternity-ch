Jobs

When I think about Rebekah Children's Services, I do not think about the number of programs we run, the range of services we provide, or even the many children and families who turn to us for help. These define what we do, not what we are.

Rebekah Children's Home was founded in 1897 as an orphanage. It received 501(c)(3) status in 1953 which allowed it to accept charitable contributions. In 1957, Rebekah Children's Services began caring for children referred by the courts because of neglect and abuse. Today it is recognized as a leading provider of mental health services for children and families throughout Santa Clara County, as well as the State of California.

Why?

Family Centered

RCS is a family centered organization, connecting children, parents, caregivers and all we serve to healthy, enduring relationships that offer them trust, safety and permanency. We hear and respond to the voice and culture of the family.

People Orientated

To me, the people of Rebekah Children's Services are the essence of RCS. What we are is not so much what we do, as it is the staff, board of directors, community advisory board, volunteers, and donors whom make it happen. I thank each and every one of you for what you have helped RCS to achieve, for what you have given to Rebekah Children's Services and to those we serve.
