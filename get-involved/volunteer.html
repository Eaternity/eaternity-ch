Volunteer With Rebekah's Children Services

Rebekah Children's Services welcomes energetic, caring and creative volunteers to help meet the needs of children and families in our community. We would be unable to provide the numerous programs and services without the skills and passion of our volunteers. There are numerous opportunities for volunteers to contribute and partner with Rebekah Children's Services. Individuals, groups or companies are invited to volunteer. Whether you offer your time, monetary support, skill set, energy, or creativity-Rebekah Children's Services will find a perfect fit for you-and we can't thank you enough!

Volunteer Opportunities
  • Fundraising Committees

    Rebekah Children's Services hosts 3-5 fundraising events throughout the year. Committee members have a wonderful time creating the theme, developing the event and hosting an experience that embraces community awareness and excitement. These events are enormously important for Rebekah Children's Services because they provide much needed funding while also educating the community about our services and programs. Currently there are three major event committees that would love to have your membership and creative support. The RCS Bowl-a-thon is usually hosted in April, RCS Casino Night is hosted in the month of August and our Festival of Trees event closes out the year in November.

    Apply Now »
  • Public Relations

    Help distribute flyers and posters in the surrounding communities. Consider helping with event or holiday invitations and mailings. Help to develop flyers; posters or media creations to promote Rebekah Children's Services.

    Apply Now »
  • Task Volunteers

    Rebekah Children's Services can always use the support of volunteers for specific tasks. This can consist of a wide range of activities such as administrative support, event servers, event caters, tree decorators (for Holiday event), guest greeters, check-in assistance, beverage servers, holiday entertainers, monthly mailings, and much more.

    Apply Now »
  • Groups, Companies or Organizations

    Wouldn't it be fun to have your group, organization or company become involved with community projects that impact the lives of so many children and families? Perhaps your organization or company would be interested in providing a fresh coat of paint for the children's cottages or volunteer to host a holiday event party or game day. Eagle scouts, or other community Clubs may choose to earn their community badges or awards by becoming involved. Rebekah Children's Services will match your area of interest and time commitment to a project that could really use your benefits.

    Apply Now »
